Our Services

At Shree Mahalaxmi Electricals & Interior, We provide a comprehensive range of services designed to meet all your interior and electrical needs.

False Ceiling

False ceilings are a popular choice in modern interior design for their aesthetic appeal. They not only conceal electrical wiring and piping but also provide excellent heat insulation. Enhance your home's beauty and functionality with a stylish false ceiling.

Painting

Home painting breathes life into your space, but with so many paint options and costs to consider, finding the perfect choice can feel overwhelming.

Exterior Building Painting

The cost of exterior building painting depends on factors like size, paint type, surface condition, and local labor rates, ensuring a tailored, high-quality finish with meticulous surface preparation and application.

Electrical Wiring

Hiring a qualified electrician ensures safe, reliable installations and repairs, protecting your property and loved ones from potential hazards.

Tiles Flooring

Flooring tiles offer a stylish range of materials, designs, and colors—such as ceramic, vitrified, and porcelain—delivering durability, low maintenance, and cost-effectiveness with endless design possibilities.

Kitchen Platform

The kitchen, a central hub of every Indian home, demands durability and elegance, making it essential to choose long-lasting materials that can withstand daily use and cleaning.

Brick Wall Making

Brick wall making artfully combines precise bricklaying techniques with creative patterns to produce both durable and visually striking structures.

Wall Plastering

Wall plastering transforms surfaces with a smooth, durable finish, setting the perfect stage for a flawless paint or wallpaper application.

Furniture Making

Furniture making is the art of crafting exquisite, functional pieces with precision and creativity, transforming raw materials into elegant designs.

Interior Demolition

Home renovation begins with the careful demolition of old elements, including brick walls, plaster, flooring, and wall tiles, setting the stage for a fresh, stunning transformation.

AC Pelmet

An AC pelmet elegantly conceals your air conditioner's piping and wiring while also incorporating the curtain rod and track, creating a seamless and stylish finish.

PCC Flooring

Plain cement concrete (PCC) flooring offers a sleek, durable, and cost-effective foundation for any space, combining simplicity with lasting quality.
